Manufacturing Procedure

Careful step-by-step process ensuring quality and effectiveness

1

Neem Preparation

Take 60g of fresh neem leaves. Clean thoroughly and grind them into a fine paste to maximize the extraction of active compounds.

2

Oil Heating

Heat 100ml of coconut oil in a clean container to optimal extraction temperature. Proper heating ensures maximum extraction of neem's active ingredients.

3

Infusion Process

Add the ground neem paste to the heated coconut oil. Stir continuously and maintain temperature to allow complete infusion of neem properties into the oil.

4

Cooling Phase

Allow the neem-infused oil mixture to cool down to room temperature. This step is crucial for maintaining the integrity of the active compounds.

5

Filtration

Filter the cooled solution through a fine mesh or cloth to remove solid particles and leaves, obtaining a clear, potent neem-infused oil.

6

Crystal Addition

Transfer the filtered solution to the final container. Add 20g of menthol crystals and stir until completely dissolved, enhancing the repellent properties.

7

Final Enhancement

Add 10ml of white odor neutralizer to the solution. Mix thoroughly to ensure even distribution. This eliminates harsh smells while maintaining effectiveness.

8

Quality Check & Packaging

Perform final quality checks for consistency, color, and odor. Once approved, the product is ready for packaging and use.
